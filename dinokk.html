Let's Unearth the . . .

Dinosaurs of Khon Kaen is a finished project in which I illustrate dinosaurs found within Thailand, specifically Khon Kaen, by using references from its related species, fossil records combined with consults from geologists to create authentic, updated artwork of dinosaurs all while maintaining artistic beauty and wonder.
